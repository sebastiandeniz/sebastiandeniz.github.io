Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Sebastian Deniz, and I am a Physics student. Here I share my active reading summaries and annotations for various books. Reading is a powerful tool that can expand our knowledge and help us grow as individuals. However, simply reading a book is not enough - actively engaging with the material and making connections with our own experiences is what truly allows us to learn and apply the insights gained from reading.

On this website, you'll find my personal summaries and notes of books across different topics. I believe that sharing our knowledge and experiences is one of the most effective ways to learn and grow. I hope my active reading notes will provide you with valuable insights and new perspectives.
